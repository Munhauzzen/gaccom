We offer Standard, Pro, and Expert copywriting services for all content formats.

All of our writers – regardless of level – are carefully vetted native English speakers with experience writing SEO-optimized copy. So, what’s the difference between each level?

  • Standard

    Hand-chosen by our demanding in-house editors.
    Only 2% of all applicants make it as a Standard copywriter.

  • Pro

    Only a few copywriters make it from Standard to Pro level. These top-quality copywriters have proven expertise in specific industries and create content that produces measurable results for our clients.

  • Expert

    With deep industry-specific knowledge, these elite copywriters produce content that positions our clients as thought leaders in their respective industries. Our Expert copywriters’ client work appears on some of the world’s top online resources.

Get A Copywriter proofreading offers more
than the standard proofreading service

Our proofreaders act as copy editors and will ensure consistency
and flow as well as the fundamentals of good copy

What’s included?

  • Correct grammar, spelling, and punctuation
  • Appropriate word usage
  • Clarity of meaning
  • Consistency of tone and style
  • Logical flow between ideas
  • Conformity to house style guidelines
  • Correct formatting for citations

Will the proofreader
change or rewrite my text?

Our proofreaders won’t alter the meaning of the text. Rather, they’ll optimize the copy to convey its message with accuracy and clarity.

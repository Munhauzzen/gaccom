Both options always come with:

All costs up front

No surprises or hidden fees, ever. You select the writer level, the word count, and get the price instantly.

Fast turnaround

Our turnaround times are short. In most cases, we’ll have your written, edited project completed within 2 to 3 days, although large projects can take longer. See the order page for details.

Top Talent

We use a rigorous vetting process to ensure we only hire talented writers. Every writer is a native English speaker with writing experience and proven skill. Learn more about our talent.

Streamlined project matching

You choose your level of involvement. Select from Smart match - a fast, reliable method to get the right writer for your project. Our editors review hundreds of projects each week and know which writer is the best match for your brief. - or - Receive pitches and choose the one you like best.

Editing on every project

Before a client ever sees it, our in-house editors review all content for responsiveness to the creative brief, quality and originality. Every submission gets pre-reviewed by at least 2 sets of eyes.

No plagiarism

All the content that we produce is 100% unique. We use Copyscape, an industry leading tool for checking originality, as well as manual editor oversight.

Unlimited revisions

We promise you’ll be satisfied. In fact, we’ve built in this promise with our unlimited revisions policy. If you aren’t happy with a project, we’ll revise it until you are.

100% money-back guarantee

If we can’t meet your expectations, you get your money back. All of it. This may not be the standard in our industry, but it’s a no-brainer for us.

Dedicated account managers

We’re here to help. Our dedicated account managers will ensure your project meets your expectations. They handle everything from project matching to writer management so you don’t have to worry about a thing.

State-of-the-art platform

Our proprietary platform is what distinguishes us from others. We handle any volume of projects and can scale with you without losing quality of work or failing deadlines. The system offers communication, project and talent management, and automations in a single convenient interface.

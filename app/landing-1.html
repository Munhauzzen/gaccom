  • Can you find me a writer with subject matter expertise?
  • With nearly 400 copywriters in our stable, we can find someone with knowledge of your kind of business. All of our content writers are carefully screened and matched by industry sector – so if you need a finance article written, your job will only be offered to copywriters with a finance industry background. Indeed, best content writers at your service.
  • What should I include in the brief so the writer understands what I need?
  • How specific must I be when placing my order?
  • It’s your choice. You can take full control over your project by specifying things like research sources, reference style, content format, local grammar, document voice, images, keywords and target audience. Or you can submit a simple order for a general subject area, and let our copywriters create something you love.
  • Are there really unlimited revisions? Does it cost extra?
  • If you don’t like the first draft, you can ask for multiple revisions at no extra charge, until you’re satisfied. Like with a dough mixture though, too much stirring can ruin the result. It may be that you need a different writer and if so, we’ll assign someone else to finish the job at your same contracted price.
  • What types of payment do you accept?
  • We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover as well as direct payments through PayPal. You have the assurance of world-class security and payment convenience.
  • Do I have to pay before I receive the complete order?
  • Yes. We escrow the full amount and ensure both you as client and the copywriter get what they came for. We manually review all orders to ensure you get the quality you expected. Besides, we make it simple to request revisions or get a full refund if you are not happy with the result.
  • Is my information confidential?
  • Yes, we make it available only to our registered copywriters, all of whom operate under a blanket Non-disclosure Agreement to keep client communications confidential. As the client, you retain all copyrights to the work product.
  • How long does it take to get a refund if I’m not happy with the result?
  • If none of our copywriters’ pitches really speak to what you are looking for, you can cancel the job and receive a full refund on your account within 24 hours. If your written copy can’t be revised to your satisfaction, you will receive a refund within 3 business days.
  • Is there anything else I need to know?
  • We offer world-class support! It's important for us that your experience with Get A Copywriter is successful, and sometimes this means you need to know we're there in your darkest hour. You can call our toll-free number 888.889.0150 if you can’t find an answer on our FAQ portal. Or, email our team at support@getacopywriter.com.
